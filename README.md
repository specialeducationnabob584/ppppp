## 🛡️ Privacy and security

Your information resides in a local folder on your computer. The application does not send your data to any remote servers. This offline design prevents unauthorized access from the internet. You represent the only person with access to these files by default.

Back up your data regularly. Locate the data folder on your computer and copy those files to an external drive or a secure cloud service. This practice ensures your information remains safe even if you replace your computer.
